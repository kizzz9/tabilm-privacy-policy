Our Approach

Tabilm (the “App”) is designed so your travel memories stay on your device and in your own iCloud — nothing else. There is no developer-operated server, and the App contains no analytics or advertising SDKs. The developer cannot see your trips or videos.

What We Handle and Where It Lives

DataWhere it lives, and why
Trip titles, dates, notes, itineraries, packing listsYour iCloud (CloudKit) and your device, to keep and sync your trips
Captured video clips and thumbnails (may include where they were shot)Your device only
Trip photos imported from your Photo LibraryYour device only; read only while you keep the toggle on
Exported VlogsSaved to your Photo Library only when you choose “Save to Camera Roll”
Free-export usage flag (one free export)Your device and your iCloud key-value storage, to restore the trial after reinstall
In-app purchase historyApple’s StoreKit; payment details are processed by Apple and never reach the App

Device Features and Permissions

  • Camera / Microphone: used only while you are filming.
  • Location: while using the App only — place search (Apple Maps), itinerary weather (coordinates sent to Apple Weather), and tagging clips with where they were shot.
  • Photo Library: reading photos you choose to import, and adding finished Vlogs (add-only).
  • Notifications: local notifications only (such as shoot reminders). There is no push server.

You can decline any permission and keep using the rest of the App.

Third-Party Services

  • Apple iCloud / CloudKit — storing, syncing and sharing trip data
  • Apple Weather (WeatherKit) — itinerary coordinates are sent to Apple to show weather
  • Apple Maps (MapKit) — search text is sent to Apple for place search
  • App Store (StoreKit) — in-app purchase

These are Apple services, and Apple’s privacy policy applies to them. No data is sent anywhere else, and we never sell or share your information with third parties.

When You Share a Trip

When you share a trip via iCloud, only the people who accept your invitation can view and edit that trip’s itinerary and packing list. Revoking the share ends their access. Captured videos stay on the owner’s device and are not uploaded to iCloud.

Deleting Your Data

  • Deleting a trip removes its data from iCloud and the videos, thumbnails and itinerary from your device.
  • You can leave a shared trip at any time.
  • Deleting the App removes all local data. Data in iCloud can be managed from Settings → Apple ID → iCloud on your device.

Children / Revisions

The App is not intended for users under 13. If we revise this policy, we will update the date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the App.
